Welcome to La Revolución, a website summarising the events of the Cuban Revolution from 1953 until the removal President Fulgencio Batista in 1959 and the aftermath. Click on one of the topics below to learn more about this period of Cuba's history.

Pre-Revolution Cuba

Fulgencio Batista, a former soldier who had served as the elected president of Cuba from 1940 to 1944, became president for the second time in 1952, after seizing power in a military coup and canceling the 1952 elections. His dictatorial regime would lay the ground work for the coming revolution
